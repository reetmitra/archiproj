## 2. Content

### About the prof
- [ ] ⚠ Bio (short + long), headshot, CV
- [ ] Links: Google Scholar, ORCID, LinkedIn, personal site

### Publications
- [ ] ⚠ Full publication list — **BibTeX export or CV is fine**
- [ ] Which 3–5 to feature on the homepage?
- [ ] Which PDFs can we legally host? (Ask about accepted-manuscript /
  open-access versions for paywalled papers)

### Research
- [ ] ⚠ How does he group his research? (Prototype guesses three themes:
  ageing in place / mobility equity / public space — react to these)
- [ ] Project list: title, 2–3 sentence description, years, status,
  collaborators, images
- [ ] ⚠ **Funding acknowledgments** — exact wording per grant (agencies often
  mandate this)

### People
- [ ] ⚠ Current members: names, roles, one-line bios, photos
- [ ] Alumni to include (name, year, where they went)
- [ ] **Photo/bio consent** confirmed for everyone listed

### Teaching
- [ ] Courses: codes, titles, terms, level, short descriptions
- [ ] Should syllabi be public?

### Join us
- [ ] Current openings (PhD, RA, etc.) and their descriptions
- [ ] How should applicants contact him, and what should they include?

### News
- [ ] ⚠ 5–10 recent items to launch with (papers, talks, grants, media,
  arrivals) — an empty feed makes the site look dead on day one
